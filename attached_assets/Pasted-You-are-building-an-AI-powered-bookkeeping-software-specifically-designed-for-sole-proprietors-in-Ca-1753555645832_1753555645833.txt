You are building an AI-powered bookkeeping software specifically designed for sole proprietors in Canada.

The software helps users automatically manage their business finances with minimal manual effort. It leverages GPT-4, OCR, and real-time financial logic to automate expense tracking, categorize transactions, and generate CRA-ready reports. It uses a subscription model costing $25 per month with a 14-day free trial and no free plan.

### Target User:
Canadian sole proprietors who manage their own business finances and need a simple, smart, and automated way to stay on top of income, expenses, receipts, and taxes.

---

## 💡 Key Features:

### 1. **AI Transaction Categorization**
- Uses GPT-4 to automatically categorize transactions based on vendor, amount, and description.
- Provides a confidence score and explanation for audit purposes.
- Stores suggestions with each transaction.

### 2. **Smart Receipt Upload & Matching**
- Users can upload images or PDFs of receipts.
- OCR (Mindee) extracts data like vendor, date, and amount.
- The system tries to automatically match the receipt to an existing transaction (exact amount/date/vendor).
- Unmatched receipts go to a review queue for manual linking.

### 3. **Bank Feed Integration**
- Connects to Canadian bank accounts using Plaid.
- Automatically imports transactions daily.
- Each imported transaction is categorized by AI and marked for review.

### 4. **Natural Language AI Chat**
- Users can ask financial questions in plain English (e.g., "How much did I spend on meals last quarter?")
- GPT-4 converts questions to SQL, queries the database, and responds with a clear summary.
- Supports queries across P&L, GST, expenses, cash flow, and monthly trends.

### 5. **Financial Reporting (CRA-Ready)**
- Profit & Loss, Balance Sheet, GST/HST Summary
- Exportable to PDF and filterable by fiscal period
- All reports formatted to meet Canadian tax requirements

### 6. **Audit Trail**
- Every AI-generated suggestion (categorization, match, chat response) is stored with time, confidence level, and optional user override.
- Full data traceability for CRA audits.

### 7. **Subscription & Access Control**
- Users receive a 14-day free trial upon signup.
- After trial ends, access is restricted unless subscribed to a paid plan.
- Stripe integration handles billing, renewals, and plan enforcement.

### 8. **Multi-Device Access + Secure Hosting**
- Responsive UI for mobile and desktop
- Canadian data residency optional but not required
- Full HTTPS and environment-based configuration
